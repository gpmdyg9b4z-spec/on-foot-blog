About This Record

These are notes from guided walking tours — transcribed, edited by Claude, and set down while the detail is still fresh. They are not academic accounts, nor official guides. They are a record of what was said, what was noticed, and what lingered.

Each post follows a walk, draws on the guide's knowledge and the transcript of the day, and is written for the reader who was there as much as the one who was not.

Tours are added as they are taken. The archive begins in London, in 2026.
